Legends on Wheels: Historic RC30 Honda to Ride into Auction History

A piece of racing history is set to rev up at auction in the UK this summer, as a rare and storied Honda VFR750R RC30 makes its way to the block. This legendary bike has a storied past, having been piloted by motorsport icons Joey Dunlop and Steve Hislop in the 1990s. Get ready to bid on a slice of motorcycle racing heritage.

A motorcycle legend is about to ride into its next chapter in the UK's summer auction season. A highly sought-after Honda VFR750R RC30 RC30 is set to be auctioned off in the coming months, carrying with it an air of nostalgia for motorsport fans and collectors alike. This exceptional model boasts a rich racing history, having had the privilege of bearing legendary riders Joey Dunlop and Steve Hislop to the starting grids of numerous high-profile events in the 1990s. These legendary two-speed heroes, as known by some, left their mark on several prestigious motorcycle racing circuits.



The VFR750R RC30 is renowned for its impressive performance and unique racing legacy. Developed by the brilliant minds at Honda, the VFR750R RC30 was created to compete in the 250cc production-based category of the World Production TT series, with Dunlop achieving multiple victories with this very model. Dunlop's racing accomplishments in the late 1980s and early 1990s are the stuff of motorcycle legend; and for those that can recall this glorious era, this particular RC30 offers more than just a chance to own a historic motorcycle – it provides a tangible connection to motorsport's rich past.

The auction, slated to take place in the UK later this summer, will undoubtedly generate high interest among motorcycle enthusiasts, collectors, and racing aficionados worldwide. When it comes to the value of this highly anticipated piece of racing history, expect the price to be substantial, considering the rarity of such a machine with such storied credentials.
