Trackhouse Racing to Tackle Post-Brivio Era in MotoGP

Davide Brivio, the highly decorated team manager of the Trackhouse MotoGP team, will be stepping down from his role at the end of 2026, marking a significant shift in the team's leadership structure. As his tenure comes to a close, fans and industry insiders alike are left wondering how the team will adapt to the void left by Brivio's departure. The search for his replacement is likely to be a contentious and highly publicized process.

**Trackhouse Racing to bid farewell to team principal Brivio**
MotoGP fans might already be aware that a major change is brewing at Trackhouse Racing. In news that's sent shockwaves throughout the MotoGP community, it's been confirmed that Davide Brivio will step down from his position as the team's principal at the end of the 2026 season. This move comes as a massive blow to the team, but Trackhouse Racing officials are keen to focus on the positives, emphasizing the need for fresh perspectives and new ideas at the top.

Brivio's departure might seem like a sudden twist, but his impending exit is the culmination of his stated intention to take a step back and focus on family and personal projects. After enjoying an illustrious career with Suzuki, Yamaha, and the Factory Ducati team, Brivio took the reins at Trackhouse Racing in 2024, guiding them to a couple of impressive podium finishes. Despite this impressive turnaround, he's always remained tight-lipped about his future plans.

Brivio's influence has been instrumental in transforming Trackhouse Racing into a genuine top-tier force in MotoGP.
The departure of the highly skilled Brivio has been met with both curiosity and concern from fans and experts. While some are optimistic about a new, rejuvenated era beginning for Trackhouse Racing, others are anxious about who will fill Brivio's significant shoes. As we hurtle into the close season, whispers regarding potential replacements for Brivio are starting to surface. Expect fireworks and intrigue as the MotoGP paddock enters this period of change and upheaval in the coming months.
